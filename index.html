5. One-of-One Protocol: Destruction Before Resale

5.1 Destruction Requirement

Before reselling any CANA NFT on the Marketplace, you must permanently destroy the corresponding physical product. This ensures that no two people ever own the same physical item, maintaining the rarity and integrity of the NFT.

5.2 Destruction Verification

To verify destruction, you must:

  • Record a clear video showing the complete destruction of the physical product, ensuring the QR code is visible in the video.
  • Submit the video through the CANA Marketplace for verification.
  • Wait for confirmation that the destruction has been verified before proceeding with the resale.

5.3 Verification Standards

Destruction verification videos must meet the following standards:

  • The entire product must be clearly visible throughout the video.
  • The QR code must be clearly visible at the beginning of the video.
  • The destruction must be complete and irreversible.
  • The video must be continuous, without cuts or edits.

CANA reserves the right to reject verification videos that do not meet these standards.

5.4 Prohibited Actions

The following actions are strictly prohibited:

  • Selling or transferring the physical product separately from the NFT.
  • Creating or distributing replicas or counterfeits of CANA products.
  • Submitting fraudulent destruction verification videos.
  • Attempting to resell an NFT without completing the Destruction Protocol.

Violation of these prohibitions may result in account suspension, NFT invalidation, and potential legal action.

7. Marketplace and Resale Engine

7.1 Resale Process

The CANA Marketplace enables the resale of RWA-Backed NFTs subject to the following conditions:

  • The seller must complete the Destruction Protocol as outlined in Section 5.
  • The NFT can only be resold through the official CANA Marketplace.
  • The resale price is set by the seller within the parameters established by CANA.

7.2 Royalty Distribution

Upon successful resale of an NFT:

  • 50% of the resale value is automatically distributed to the original seller (previous owner)
  • 50% of the resale value is retained by CANA as a resale royalty, which funds the reproduction of a new physical item for the buyer

These distributions are executed automatically through smart contracts and cannot be modified or circumvented.
